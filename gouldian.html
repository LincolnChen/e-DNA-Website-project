The Gouldian finch

1. The Gouldian finch is a brightly coloured finch found in northern Australia.

⇨

The Gouldian finch

2. It inhabits areas from the Kimberley area in Western Australia, through the Top End to Cape Yorke in northern Queensland.

⇦
⇨

The Gouldian finch

3. However, the Gouldian finch is difficult to find because there are not many left in the wild.

⇦
⇨

The Gouldian finch

4. Destruction of Gouldian finch habitat due to clearing land for building roads, houses and farms, along with habitat destruction by feral animals and fires have led to the Gouldian finch becoming endangered.
